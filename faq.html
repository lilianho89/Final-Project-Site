Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a custom website take?
The time frame for custom designed sites varies depending on the scope of the project, how much information is readily available by you when we start, and how available you are to provide information and feedback. For example, the basic wordpress package might take less than a week while the gold package might take 3 weeks. You should generally allow 1-2 months for your project to be completed with revisions.
Do you work only with local clients?
No. While I have worked with many local clients, I have also worked with international clients as well. Whether you live in England, Latin America or China – we can collaborate. With today’s technology, geography is not an obstacle.
Do you offer payment plans?
All projects require an initial, non-refundable 25% deposit to hold a place in my schedule. Your booking will not be confirmed until this deposit has been received. Another 25% is due upon design approval. Please note that no work is commenced until the 50% project payment is received in full. The last 50% is due upon completion of the project.
What methods of payment do you accept?
I accept credit cards, debit cards, and e-check payments through Paypal.  A Paypal account is not required to checkout.
Do you offer web hosting?
Not at this time, but I can give you some recommendations if you need an affordable and reliable web host.
